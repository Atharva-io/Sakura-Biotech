AI-Enabled Algae Bioreactor for Consistent, Scalable Cultivation

Automate cultivation, reduce variability, and generate actionable data so every batch is measurable and repeatable.

Live Monitoring Recipe Control Batch Reports Alerts Multi-Tank View
01

What it is

A sensor-driven photobioreactor (PBR) designed to grow microalgae in a controlled, contamination-aware environment.

It combines robust cultivation hardware with software + AI insights to improve reliability and productivity whether you're running R&D, pilot production, or multi-tank distributed nodes.

02

What you can do with it

  • R&D and Strain Screening: Compare light recipes, nutrient strategies, and CO2 regimes faster without guesswork.
  • Biomass / Lipids / Pigments Production: Run consistent cycles and identify the parameters that drive yield.
  • Carbon Utilization Experiments: Measure the effect of CO2 dosing on growth performance and stability.
  • Education & Training: A complete data-first platform for hands-on algae cultivation and process learning.
03

Why teams choose this system

  • Stable Control: Maintain temperature, pH/EC (if used), DO/CO2, light intensity, and mixing within target bands.
  • Repeatable Batches: Standardized recipes + logs reduce dependence on manual operator routines.
  • Faster Learning: Clear trends and event timelines show why a batch performed well or poorly.
  • Scale by Replication: Add more tanks/nodes without losing visibility, control, or traceability.
04

Core system modules

  1. Cultivation Hardware: Reactor/tank geometry optimized for light delivery and mixing; gas exchange + CO2 dosing options; harvest-friendly plumbing and modular expansion.
  2. Lighting & Photoperiod Control: Programmable photoperiod schedules; intensity control and saved light recipes; strain-specific modes (growth vs. target compounds).
  3. Sensor Stack (Configurable): Temperature, pH/EC (when relevant), DO, CO2, ORP, light intensity, and more; data validation + alerts for drift, outliers, or sensor failure.
  4. Automation & Control: Closed-loop or rule-based actuator control (mixing, aeration, CO2, dosing); fail-safes, thresholds, and event logging; manual override with safe operating limits.
  5. Data Platform & AI Insights: Live dashboard + batch history; trend graphs, alarms, and recipe-to-outcome comparisons; optional predictive analytics for early instability detection.
05

How it works

  1. Configure a recipe: Set light schedule, setpoints, control limits, and operating rules.
  2. Run a batch: Sensors stream data continuously and the system logs key events.
  3. Monitor & optimize: Alerts and trends highlight drift, instability, or unusual behavior early.
  4. Harvest & review: Automated batch reports summarize performance drivers and key changes.
  5. Improve next cycle: Refine recipes based on measured results and repeat with confidence.
06

Who it's for

  • Microalgae startups and pilot plants
  • Research labs and universities
  • Aquafeed / biofertilizer / bioactive ingredient teams
  • Carbon utilization and climate-tech experiments
07

What you can track

  • Growth stability (day-to-day consistency)
  • Yield per batch and per unit energy
  • Impact of light recipes and CO2 dosing strategies
  • Contamination signals (early warning indicators)
  • Process repeatability across multiple tanks/nodes

Mushroom Grow Box for Consistent, Clean Harvests

A compact controlled environment that stabilizes humidity, temperature, and fresh-air exchange so your grows are repeatable with less daily effort.

Stable Humidity CO2 / Fresh-Air Control Clean Design Scalable Boxes
01

What it is

A smart mushroom grow box that creates a reliable microclimate for legal gourmet and medicinal mushrooms.

It combines controlled airflow, humidity management, and monitoring to reduce the most common causes of failed or inconsistent grows.

(Designed for legal cultivation only - culinary and medicinal species.)

02

What you can do with it

  • Home & Hobby Growing: Grow consistently without constantly checking conditions or misting all day.
  • Small-Scale Production: Run repeatable cycles to supply kitchens, cafes, and local customers.
  • R&D and Strain Trials: Compare substrates, spawn rates, or environmental recipes in a controlled setup.
  • Training & Education: A simple, visible system to learn cultivation fundamentals with real data.
03

Why growers choose this system

  • Stable Humidity (without over-wetting): Maintain target RH and reduce pooling, soggy blocks, and contamination risk.
  • Fresh-Air Exchange & CO2 Management: Avoid long-stem and low-quality fruiting caused by CO2 buildup.
  • Repeatable Results: Save and follow stable routines so outcomes do not depend on constant manual effort.
  • Clean, Practical Design: Easy-to-wipe surfaces and organized airflow help maintain hygiene and reduce failure points.
04

Core system modules

  1. Grow Chamber (Box Design): Compact, sealed structure with controlled openings; layout designed for predictable airflow and uniform conditions; easy access for loading and unloading blocks or trays.
  2. Humidity Control: Maintains target RH with smart cycling; designed to reduce over-misting and wet spots; supports stable fruiting conditions.
  3. Airflow / Fresh-Air Exchange: Controlled intake and exhaust behavior to manage CO2 and oxygen; supports different growth phases (colonization vs fruiting) through settings.
  4. Monitoring & Alerts: Tracks key environmental conditions (temperature, humidity, optional CO2); alerts for out-of-range conditions; basic logs to learn what changes affected results.
  5. Recipe / Mode Settings (Optional): Simple modes for typical phases; repeatable settings for different species and seasons; manual override for full control.
05

How it works

  1. Load your grow: Place your legal mushroom grow blocks or trays inside the chamber.
  2. Select a mode or settings: Choose target humidity, airflow, and temperature range for your species.
  3. System maintains the environment: Humidity and air exchange run automatically to keep conditions stable.
  4. Monitor and adjust: Use readings and alerts to fine-tune based on growth response.
  5. Harvest and repeat: Stable conditions give repeatable flushes and easier planning.
06

Who it's for

  • Beginners who want a simpler, more reliable setup
  • Home growers focused on consistent results
  • Small businesses producing gourmet mushrooms
  • Labs, educators, and training programs
  • Anyone who wants to reduce contamination and environmental guesswork
07

What you can track

  • Humidity stability over time (and how it impacts fruiting)
  • Temperature consistency during different seasons
  • CO2 trends and fresh-air performance (if included)
  • Batch repeatability (flush timing and yield stability)
  • Failure reduction signals (drift alerts and instability periods)

A recirculating hydroponics system with monitoring and optional automation so your plants stay stable, your nutrients stay on-point, and your results are repeatable.

pH/EC Monitoring Recirculating Reservoir Modular Expansion Automation-Ready
01

What it is

A modular hydroponics platform designed for controlled, efficient plant cultivation without soil.

It delivers nutrients directly to the root zone using a clean recirculating system, supported by monitoring and optional dosing automation to keep your crop stable across seasons and batches.

02

What you can do with it

  • Home & Indoor Growing: Grow herbs and leafy greens with less mess and more control than soil.
  • Commercial Trials & Small Production: Run consistent cycles for repeatable yield and predictable planning.
  • Research & Controlled Experiments: Test lighting, nutrient recipes, and environmental changes with measurable data.
  • Nursery & Propagation: Maintain stable conditions for seedlings and clones to improve survival and uniformity.
03

Why growers choose this system

  • Precise Nutrient Control: Maintain optimal pH and EC ranges so plants absorb nutrients efficiently.
  • Cleaner & More Efficient: Recirculating design reduces water waste and avoids soil-borne variability.
  • Repeatable Results: Log conditions and follow stable routines with less dependence on gut feel.
  • Scales Easily: Add more grow sites or modules without redesigning your entire setup.
04

Core system modules

  1. Grow Modules (Crop Layout): Configurable layouts (channels, buckets, or crop-specific designs); root-zone design focused on uniform delivery; modular add-ons for expanding capacity.
  2. Reservoir + Recirculation: Central reservoir with pump-driven circulation; stable flow design for consistent nutrient delivery; easy maintenance access for cleaning and refills.
  3. Monitoring Stack (Recommended): pH monitoring; EC/TDS monitoring; temperature monitoring; alerts for drift and out-of-range conditions.
  4. Dosing & Automation (Optional): Rule-based or scheduled dosing; safety limits and manual override; event logs for traceability.
  5. Dashboard & Data Logs: Live status view of key parameters; trends over time; batch comparison; exportable logs for reporting and analysis.
05

How it works

  1. Choose your crop and layout: Select a system design based on plant type and available space.
  2. Prepare nutrient solution: Mix nutrients in the reservoir and set initial pH and EC targets.
  3. System circulates automatically: Nutrient solution is delivered and recirculated continuously or on a schedule.
  4. Monitor and stabilize: Sensors track drift and help you maintain optimal ranges consistently.
  5. Harvest and repeat: Stable conditions produce repeatable growth cycles and cleaner planning.
06

Who it's for

  • Home growers and indoor farming setups
  • Herb and leafy green production
  • Research labs and universities
  • Controlled-environment agriculture teams
  • Anyone who wants measurable, repeatable plant growth
07

What you can track

  • pH stability (drift over time)
  • EC/TDS stability (nutrient strength control)
  • Water usage and refill frequency
  • Growth cycle consistency across batches
  • Recipe performance (what nutrient strategy produced better results)
